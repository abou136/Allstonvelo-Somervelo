All Star Bicycle Service in Allston Rock City

Allstonvelo is a unique local bike shop offering a sense of comfort and familiarity to a burgeoning cycling community. It's important to us that your bicycle receives the same attention to detail and integrity that you've come to expect from the best bike shop in Boston. Visit us to enjoy a refreshingly casual atmosphere and receive meticulous mechanical service that we are known for.

Craftsmanship Creates Confidence You Can Count On

We make a point to thoroughly discuss the service of every bicycle we service with approachable, informative expertise. It's important to us that you ride out of Allstonvelo with the same comprehensive level of insight and understanding about your bike that we apply to the work we perform. We reimagined the bike shop to give you the confidence you need to navigate the streets and concentrate on what's ahead of you, while putting miles behind you.
